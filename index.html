About Us

Welcome to Team 9589, a passionate community of students and mentors dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation in robotics. Based in MCS in Deep River, ON, our team thrives on collaboration, creativity, and a commitment to excellence. Together, we tackle real-world engineering challenges and inspire future leaders in STEM fields.

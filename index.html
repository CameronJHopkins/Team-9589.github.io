About Us

Welcome to Team 9589, a passionate community of students and mentors dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation in robotics. Based in MCS in Deep River, ON, our team thrives on collaboration, creativity, and a commitment to excellence. Together, we tackle real-world engineering challenges and inspire future leaders to pursue careers in technology/engineering, computer science, and business.

Our Sponsors

Gold Sponsors

Renfrew County District School Board

The Renfrew County District School Board (RCDSB) educates over 10,000 students in 28 schools and is the third largest employer in the county. They celebrate the unique contributions of each individual, and cultivate an environment that values imagination and innovation. To learn more, visit their website.








Silver Sponsors

Bubble Technologies Industries

Located in Chalk River, Canada, BTI is a technology firm that provides advanced commercial, homeland security, and defence solutions in radiation, explosives, and contraband detection. Systems developed by BTI have flown on more than twenty space missions and the company’s technology has been used around the world by groups including the Canadian Department of National Defence, US Department of Defense, US Department of Homeland Security, US Department of Energy, NATO forces, the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), and law enforcement/national security agencies. Founded in 1988, the company has a highly-skilled staff of over 50 people with expertise in physics, chemistry, electronics, mechanical design, software/algorithm development, and manufacturing. The company is internationally recognized for its innovative solutions for some of the world’s most complex radiation-related applications. For more, visit their website.








Bronze Sponsors

Canadian Nuclear Laboratories

CNL is Canada’s premier nuclear science and technology organization, and a world leader in developing nuclear technology for peaceful and innovative applications. Using their unique expertise, they are restoring and protecting the environment, they are advancing clean energy technology, and their medical breakthroughs continue to improve the health of people around the world. For more, visit their website.








Lazarus Electric

Lazarus Electric has been exceeding the needs of the Ottawa Valley since 2018! Their team of electricians specialize in residential renovations, service calls, and commercial retrofits. For more, visit their website.








Argosy Foundation

The mission of the Argosy Foundation is to support people and programs that make our society a better place to live. They seek to employ creative and entrepreneurial approaches that help people to help themselves, and become self-sustaining whenever possible. Their intention is to solve systemic problems, build teams and communities, create replicable solutions, and inspire others to contribute in their own ways. For more, visit their website.








FIRST Canada

FIRST® inspires young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership. For more, visit their website.








Thoth Technology

Thoth Technology specializes in services and equipment for space applications, including remote sensing, environmental monitoring and exploration. Thoth's innovative and affordable solutions benefit our clients and global community. For more, visit their website.
